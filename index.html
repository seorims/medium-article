My Cat is On Fire!

The goal is not to try to eat them after.

It all started with a jump.

Many cats have this habit of jumping up to high places, and the stove is no exception.
While the cat is safe when there is no fire on, it can very easily burn itself if we leave our cooking alone.
But to some, it might be tempting to see a fried cat, like this person right here,
but we all know the goal is never to think about trying to eat one. How cruel!

Pray to the heavens for all cats

In recent news, criminals have been on the run and we advise people to keep their cats safe -
from fires, and of course, humans (who wish to eat fried cat).

Last but not least...

Cherish your cats, because they might just leave you one day.
